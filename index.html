About Me

I'm Naman Mehta, a data engineer and applied data scientist with a passion for leveraging cutting-edge technologies to solve complex business challenges.

With a Master's in Applied Data Science from the University of Chicago and experience at companies like JLL and Pangea Tech, I specialize in building robust data pipelines, implementing machine learning solutions, and creating insightful visualizations.
